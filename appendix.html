Appendix I: Declaration of Trustees

We, the undersigned, being the first Trustees of the ACMC 2006 Alumni Association, hereby solemnly declare as follows:

1. Adoption of Constitution

We affirm that we have read and understood the Constitution of the Association and hereby adopt it as the governing instrument for the administration of the Association.

2. Non-Profit Status

We declare that the Association is a non-profit organization. No part of the income or property of the Association shall be paid or transferred, directly or indirectly, to any member, except in furtherance of the aims and objectives of the Association.

3. Legal Compliance

We undertake to ensure that the Association shall operate in compliance with all applicable laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, including the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) 2020.

4. Trustees' Responsibilities

We acknowledge that, as Trustees, we are responsible for:

  1. Overseeing the management and proper administration of the Association;
  2. Safeguarding the property and funds of the Association;
  3. Ensuring that the objectives of the Association are pursued in good faith and in accordance with this Constitution.

5. Declaration of Truthfulness

We declare that the information provided herein, and in connection with the registration of the Association, is true, accurate, and complete to the best of our knowledge.
