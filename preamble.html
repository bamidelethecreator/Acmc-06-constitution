Preamble

We, the alumni of African Church Model College (ACMC), Ifako Ijaiye, Class of 2006, united by shared history and common purpose, do hereby establish this Constitution to foster lifelong bonds, promote personal and collective growth, support our alma mater's legacy and future, and uphold the values that shaped us.

This Constitution is established to provide a clear framework for governance, accountability, and the orderly conduct of the affairs of the Organization, in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

In furtherance of our mission, we affirm our dedication to transparency, inclusiveness, and the promotion of initiatives that contribute positively to education, social development, and community empowerment.

We therefore solemnly resolve to uphold the principles and provisions contained herein and to conduct all activities of the Organization in a manner consistent with its stated aims and objectives.
