Article 3

MEMBERSHIP

This Article establishes the categories, eligibility, rights, obligations, and disciplinary provisions relating to membership of the Association in accordance with this Constitution.

Section 1: Eligibility

Membership in the Association shall be open to individuals who meet the following criteria:

  • Any person who was officially registered as a student of African Church Model College for any period whatsoever during the academic sessions commencing 2000/2001 and ending 2005/2006.
  • Must demonstrate good character and commitment to the objectives of the Alumni Association.
  • Must satisfy any additional requirements as may be determined by the General Assembly.

Section 2: Rights and Responsibilities

2.1: Rights

Members shall have the right to:

  • Attend and participate in General Assembly meetings.
  • Vote in elections and on matters affecting the Association.
  • Stand for election to any office within the Association.
  • Receive information about the Association's activities, programmes, and finances.

2.2: Responsibilities

Members responsibility shall be to:

  • Uphold the values, objectives, and rules of the Association.
  • Pay any membership dues or levies as determined by the General Assembly
  • Participate actively in alumni programmes, events, and initiatives.
