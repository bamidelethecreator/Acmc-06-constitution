Article 1

GENERAL PROVISIONS

This Article establishes the Association's identity, legal status, objectives, guiding principles, and approved communication channels in accordance with applicable laws.

Section 1: Name

The name of the association shall be African Church Model College (ACMC), Ifako Ijaye Class 2006 Alumni Association (Hereinafter referred to as ACMC 06 Alumni).

Section 2: Motto

Heaven's light our guide

Section 3: Legal Status

  • The association has the status of a legal person and is subject to the jurisdiction and protection of the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.
  • The Nigerian laws shall govern all of its activities in accordance with the rules and regulations regarding the registration of the association.
  • The association shall be a non-political and non-profit seeking organisation.
  • The constitution and other bye-laws, rules, and regulations enacted shall be binding on each member as long as they remain in effect.

Section 4: Vision and Mission

4.1: Vision

To build a vibrant, connected, and supportive community of ACMC Class of 2006 alumni.

4.2: Mission

To foster lifelong connections, support members' personal and professional growth, uphold the legacy of ACMC, and contribute to the development of the school and its current students.

Section 5: Aims and Objectives

The objectives of the Association are to:

  • Maintain and strengthen bonds among alumni.
  • Support the growth and development of The African Church Model College.
  • Organise events, reunions, and initiatives that promote unity.
  • Provide platforms for networking, mentorship, and career support.
  • Encourage charitable activities and projects that give back to the school and community.
  • Preserve the history, values, and traditions of the Class of 2006 and ACMC at large.

Section 6: Digital Communication Channels

The Association shall utilise the following digital platforms to support its objectives and enhance member engagement:

  • Email:
    Shall be used for official notices, correspondence, meeting invitations, and formal communications between the Executive Council and members.
  • WhatsApp Community:
    Official Group Name - ACMC'06 Alumni shall be used for general updates, informal discussions, event coordination, and quick dissemination of information to members.
  • Media Accounts:
    The Association shall maintain official social media pages to promote visibility, share achievements, and connect with members and the public:
    Facebook: ACMC 06 Alumni
    Instagram: @ACMC06Alumni
    TikTok: @ACMC06Alumni
  • Cloud Storage (Google Drive or Equivalent):
    Shall be used for storing and sharing official documents, photos, reports, and archival materials relevant to the Association's activities. Access shall be limited to authorised officers of the Executive Council.

Note: The Communications Team, headed by the PRO, under the supervision of the Executive Council, shall manage all digital platforms, ensuring proper moderation, security, and compliance with the Association's communication policies. The Executive Council reserves the right to add, modify, or discontinue the use of any digital platform as may be necessary to improve efficiency or adapt to technological changes.
